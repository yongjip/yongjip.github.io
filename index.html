About

I work across data engineering, analytics, and product.

My core background is in data systems, but AI coding agents have expanded how I ship. I use them to build lightweight services and internal tools when a business problem needs an end-to-end solution, not just a dashboard or a pipeline.

  • Built and operated 100+ mart tables and 50+ dashboards in a lean team
  • Prefer serverless systems with Athena, Lambda, Glue, EMR Serverless, and DynamoDB
  • Focus on systems that reduce manual analysis and improve decision speed

Philosophy

Simple systems win more often than complicated ones.

Most high-impact data problems do not need a grand platform rewrite. They need a clean structure, reliable validation, and a workflow that people can actually maintain. I prefer systems that are easy to iterate on, easy to explain, and strong enough to support real operations.

Complex problems can often be solved with well-designed simple systems.
